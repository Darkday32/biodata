Update notes after a consultation

Whether it is a clinic or online consultation, your can add review notes about the patient about their session, for future reference. These notes can be kept private or shared with your patients.

Fill in prescription details

Consultation notes also include e-prescriptions. Review your patient's Personal Health Records, including their medication history, and make suitable changes to their existing prescription or add a new prescription

Add invoices

You can also add your service changes or consultation costs to the Consultation Notes to share with your patients. Invoices for your services will be emailed to patients for them to pay online or during their next visit.

Define follow-up action

Create actionable plans for your patients' care. You can share health goals, subscribe patients to a remote monitoring care plan that you create or even schedule a follow-up appointment for them to check back in with you.

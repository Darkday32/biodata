Electronic Medical Records

A comprehensive electronic medical record solution that helps you record every aspect of a patient’s health. Capture the patient’s details, health conditions, allergies, medications, vaccinations, surgeries, hospitalizations, social history, family history, contraindications and more. A set of 50+ health trackers with graphs that help you record and visualize patient’s data. The health trackers cover Vital Signs, Blood, Lipid Profile, Thyroid, Kidney, Electrolytes, Liver, Lung, Bone, Symptoms, Fitness and Well Being and Skin.

Medical Device Integrations

Patients can sync their data from various connected health devices into their patient health record, allowing them to collaborate better in their health. The patient’s mobile app which is available on iOS and Android is integrated with various devices and other Health Apps like Google Fit and Apple Health, allowing patients to update their data anytime, anywhere.

E-Prescriptions

Creating prescriptions is fast and easy. Frequently Prescribed Medication lists provide intelligent recommendations to save you time. Share the prescriptions with patients who can view them securely via the patient portal with printing and PDF downloads. Customize the e-prescriptions with your clinic’s branding and all the necessary details to create the perfect prescription. Track all your prescriptions and search for them by provider or patient.

Medical Imaging and Medical Reports

Medical images and scans can be attached to each patient’s medical record. We support a large number of file formats including DICOM images. Delight patients by sharing reports and patients can access them securely via the patient portal or iOS/Android patent mobile app.

Appointment Scheduling

The Biodata Virtual Practice comes integrated with a powerful clinic appointment scheduling software module complete with email/sms/push notifications to patients and providers. Exercise complete control over each doctor’s calendar and can define their services and timings, non-working days. You get a choice of Calendar and List views to confirm, reschedule and cancel patient appointment bookings. Automated appointment reminders are sent out to patients to ensure no-shows are kept down to a minimum.

Online Appointment Booking with Payments

You don’t need separate software to accept online patient appointment bookings. It is baked right into the Virtual Practice. Set the price for each service that your providers offer and patients are led to an easy to follow payment flow to complete the appointment booking. Patients can book appointments from the web patient portal or the iOS/Android patient mobile apps. The online appointment books show up directly in the appointment scheduler, so managing them is a breeze.

Medical Billing and Revenue Management

Manage the catalogue of medical services that are offered by your clinic, defining the pricing, tax and other details. You can also define the list of other billable services/offerings that are offered by your clinic. The power Clinic Billing module helps you generate bills/invoices with PDF download and printing capabilities. Powerful reports help you track your clinic revenues. Patients can view and downloads their invoices through the secure patent portal and patient mobile app.

Accept Online Patient Payments

The Virtual Practice provides out-of-box integrations with payment gateways in multiple countries. Accepting online patient payments is as easy as configuring your payment account and the rest is handled for you. Your clinic can accept online payments for both in-person consultations and for the various online healthcare services you offer, with the payments flowing directly into your bank account.

Telehealth Video Conferencing

The Biodata Virtual Practice is the easiest way to start video telehealth consultations with your patients. Unlike generic video consultation solutions like Zoom or WhatsApp, the Virtual Practice is software that is specifically developed for telehealth consultations and provides everything you need for safe, secure and HIPAA compliant video calls with your patients. Choose a workflow that matches your requirements and the application supports both online appointment based flows or no-login waiting room based flows. Online payment integrations are available out-of-the-box, allowing you to set pricing for each service that your providers offer. The automated notifications and appointment reminders minimize the no-shows. Everything you need to grow your clinic revenues, available in one convenient package.

Telehealth Text Consultations

The Virtual Practice provides a wider range of telehealth options. So if you are looking for a simpler form of paid telehealth consultations, the text consultation software module is a perfect solution. Highly customizable with per provider pricing, online payment integrations, notifications/alerts it is a perfect form of asynchronous telemedicine, that works for busy providers who are pressed for time.

Remote Patient Monitoring

Preventive care and remote patient monitoring is the future of healthcare. The Biodata Virtual Practice includes a complete Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM) software module that enables you to generating revenues today. Create customizable care plans, monitoring teams, track patient data across 50+ health trackers and provide patients with feedback on their data. Breach alerts help you take quick action if specified limits are exceeded. Engage patient with notifications and nudge them to stay on track with adherence alerts. The application integrates with various health apps like Apple Watch/Apple Health Kit, Google Fit allowing patients to sync their data into a care plan. Bluetooth health devices integrations also allow patients to send their Blood Sugar, Blood Pressure, SPO2, Temperature, Pulse and Weight readings directly from their connected health devices directly to their doctor with a few clicks.

Medical Practice Website

Get a marketing website for your clinic on a domain name of your choice (e.g. www.yourclinicname.com). The website is custom designed for medical practices and is closely integrated with all the features you need to take your clinic or medical practice online. It includes online appointment booking for in-person and video telehealth appointments, text consultation telehealth engagement and remote patient monitoring. Patients can book any of your services online and make a payment using their credit or debit cards. The practice marketing website also provides a provider listing for your patients with powerful search capabilities and an integrated health blog for Patient Education.

Mobile Apps for Patients

Your patients can engage with you via the iOS and Android native patient mobile apps that are available for download in the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. The patient mobile apps provide range of functionality including Patient Health Records, Online Appointment Booking and Management, Remote Patent Monitoring, Online Payments, Telehealth Video Consultations, Text Consultations and other functionality

Apps for Providers

Healthcare Providers also get their own free iOS and Android apps that provide all the essential patient management and consultation features allowing them to manage everything on the go. Patient records, telehealth video conferencing, text consultations, remote patent monitoring and a lot more.

Patient Engagement and Notifications

One-on-one or group patient engagement is now easy and secure. Automated notifications to both patients and providers are tightly integrated with all key features like appointment booking, video consultations, online payments, remote patient monitoring and text consultations. Appointment reminders and care plan adherence notifications are also built-in. Notifications work across multiple channels Email, SMS and Mobile Push.

Patient Education

The integrated Health Blog is the ideal place to publish patient educational and other marketing material related to your medical practice. The Health Blog is integrated with your marketing website and is also available to patients via the patient mobile application. You can also use the notifications feature to send out notifications regarding new content that you publish.

Reporting

Powerful reporting capabilities help you keep you track of various aspects of your business. You can track patient registrations, medical billing and revenue and online video consultations.

HIPAA, GDPR Compliant Secure Health Cloud

The Virtual Practice is HIPAA and GDPR compliant with all your data being stored in AWS cloud data centers. Based on cutting edge software architecture practices, our cloud practice management software is available across the globe with minimal access latency. Security and privacy are of paramount importance to us - in the applications, their deployment and in our operations - we are constantly working on ensuring that we are in compliance with evolving security measures. All your data is safe and secure with automated real-time data back-ups across multiple data centers, ensuring that your data is never lost.
